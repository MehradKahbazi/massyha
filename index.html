My Work

I have over 7 years of local and international experience working in the areas of trauma, addiction in remission, anxiety, depression, family and relationship conflict, self-esteem, struggles with immigration & racial identity. I aim to offer a warm and welcoming therapeutic relationship that is collaborative, inclusive, culturally sensitive, embodied/somatic and at times playful to support your growth and self-discovery. Offering a sense of curiosity and non-judgment is essential to my practice.
